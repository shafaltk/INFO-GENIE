    About Us



    LBS College of Engineering, Kasaragod is a notable engineering college in Kerala, established in 1993. It was the first self-financing engineering college in the government sector in the state and is now one of two self-financing engineering colleges under the LBS Centre for Science & Technology, Thiruvananthapuram.

    The college offers six B.Tech. programs and three M.Tech. programs with specializations in Computer Science and Information Security, Power Systems and Power Electronics, VLSI Design and Signal Processing. The programs are approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and four B.Tech. programs are accredited by NBA. The college has a sprawling 52-acre campus located in Muliyar, 10 kilometers away from the district headquarters, and is easily accessible via Mangalore Airport, which is 70 kilometers away.

    LBS College of Engineering is one of the few colleges in Kerala to be selected under the FAB LAB Scheme, a joint venture with Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA, and the Centre for Govt's Continuing Education Scheme - Additional Skill Acquisition Programme (ASAP). It has also been selected by the Kerala Government for setting up an Incubation Center, as it has helped to mold more than 50 successful entrepreneurs.

    The college has an impressive placement record, with students being placed in renowned companies like CTS, IBM, Infosys, MRF, and Saint-Gobain, among others. It also provides coaching for competitive examinations like GATE and UPSC. LBSCEK has won several awards and accolades, including the Best Engineering College Award from the Government of Kerala in 2007.
